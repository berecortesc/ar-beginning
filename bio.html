Berenice is a multidisciplinary artist who explores tension release and displacement of otherworlds in real world situations. She blends the mystery created from folklore with typical world situations to create a supernatural world. Her evolution of folklore exploration allowed her to blend new media and traditional media to weave stories in everyday scenerios. Her further exploration of new media has played a critical role in her recent pieces. In her recent pieces she envelopes what mystery means and how it can be released with the aid of colorful pastel colors. With colorful shades a blend of new media and traditional media she seeks to design an immersive world to hide from the real world.

ENTER THE 90's

Weirdos Chat is a website designed on the 90's asthetic of broken links, random information, and the classic: Chat Room.
One of my first experiences with the world wide web was using chat rooms to meet friends. I remember there being many different
types and it was an easy access. I aimed at bringing nostalgia for the 90's and so I decided to create a chat room of my own with
minimum code and built only on html. I used Adobe Fireworks, html, and videos to bring together humor, broken links, and the
difference of our technological world today vs. when the internet first began.

CODE

It (1400px by 800px), was the culmating project for this section. I used the a Pixel Processing algorithm to load in the image pixels
into Processing. Once I loaded the pixels I used pointillize to give it the "pixel" affect. I pulled the green and blue RGB values
from the image to recreate the picture with only those green and blue pixel values. I wanted to load the Pixels utilizing: createImage(),
to use a bigger canvas instead I made the picture larger in Photoshop. From there, I utilized mills() to add a pattern and previous code to
manipulate circles, color, and rectangles. Finally, with the help of frameRate(200) and saveFrame() the video was finalized. The final image
rendered for about 15 mins and it is the infamous clown, It.

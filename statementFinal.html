I am a multidisciplinary artist in digital media art exploring augmented reality, 3-D modeling, and arduino. My work focuses on new media and the different ways in which these practices can be intertwined with real world situations. I explore the mystery of the otherworld and real world to create pieces to release tension. My work includes net art and arduino pieces in combination with traditional practices together. In addition,my work releases tension through the use of bright, pastel colors to displace supernatural worlds in the real world. Currently, my work has been exhibited at San Jose State University.
